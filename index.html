Zoo Animals

Bears

  • Ollie

    • Ollie is a bear with a peculiar talent for breakdancing. Whenever the zoo plays music, Ollie can't resist busting out some moves. He's known for his signature spin, which leaves visitors both amazed and slightly dizzy.

  • Mona

    • Mona is an artistic bear with a passion for painting. Her masterpieces are created with a splash of mud here and a swipe of berry juice there. She even had a gallery showing last summer, with her abstract mud art fetching quite the attention.

Giraffes

  • Frankie

    • Frankie is a giraffe with an extraordinary necktie collection. Every morning, he carefully selects a tie to match his spots, making him the most dapper giraffe in the savannah. His favorite tie is a bright red one with tiny giraffe patterns.

  • Coconut

    • Coconut is a giraffe who loves tropical fruit, especially coconuts. He has a knack for cracking them open with his long legs, much to the delight of the zookeepers. Visitors often see him munching on his favorite treat, wearing a satisfied smile.

Lions

  • Mella

    • Mella is a lioness with a love for singing. Her roars sound more like operatic performances, and she often puts on shows for the other animals. Her powerful voice can be heard echoing across the zoo, making her the star of the feline choir.

  • Karl

    • Karl is a lion with an unusual hobby: knitting. His large paws deftly handle the needles, and he's created a variety of cozy scarves and blankets for his pride. Karl's knitting club is the most exclusive group in the zoo.

Monkeys

  • Cookie

    • Cookie is a mischievous monkey with a sweet tooth. She has a talent for sneaking into the zoo kitchen and stealing cookies. The zookeepers have given up trying to stop her and instead leave a daily batch of treats just for her.

  • Earl

    • Earl is a monkey with a fascination for human gadgets. He's particularly fond of smartphones and often mimics taking selfies with imaginary devices. Visitors love watching him strike poses and pretend to text.

  • Banana Pudding

    • Banana Pudding is the clown of the monkey troop. His antics include slipping on imaginary banana peels and making funny faces at visitors. His sense of humor is contagious, and he's always the center of attention.

Alligators

  • Wren

    • Wren is an alligator with a love for birdwatching. He spends his days lounging by the water with a pair of binoculars, carefully observing the feathered residents of the zoo. His favorite bird is the flamingo, which he finds particularly fascinating.

  • Aspen

    • Aspen is an alligator with a passion for gardening. He has a beautiful garden filled with water lilies and other aquatic plants. Aspen's green thumb (or claw) is legendary, and his pond is the most serene spot in the zoo.

  • Mika

    • Mika is an alligator with a flair for fashion. She loves accessorizing with anything shiny she can find. Whether it's a discarded piece of jewelry or a sparkling stone, Mika always manages to look glamorous. Her stylish appearances are the talk of the zoo.
